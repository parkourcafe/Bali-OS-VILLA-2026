# ICP and Scoring — Selena Systems GTM v1.0

## Final offer
**Villa Ops Response System** — AI guest inquiry, qualification and follow-up system for Bali villa management companies.

## Primary ICP
- Bali villa management company or multi-property rental operator.
- Ideally 20–60 managed villas.
- Minimum 15 premium villas or approximately 30 standard villas.
- At least 2 people handling reservations or guest inquiries.
- Preferably 150–300+ direct inquiry conversations per month.
- Meaningful direct inquiry flow through WhatsApp, website, Instagram, email, or direct booking.
- Internationally oriented or premium segment.
- Owner, GM, operations director, or head of reservations accessible.
- No obvious advanced internal AI response system already in place.

Companies below this threshold may qualify only for a smaller pilot.

## Disqualifiers
- No meaningful direct inquiry flow.
- Below threshold and no pilot economics.
- No access to owner, GM, operations director, or reservations lead.
- Already has a mature AI response system.
- Requires custom PMS integration in the first 14-day scope.
- Wants revenue, occupancy, legal, medical, or guaranteed booking claims.
- Local contracting path unresolved and no suitable partner/entity route confirmed.

## Desktop score: maximum 80 points
- Portfolio fit: 0–15.
- Premium/revenue fit: 0–10.
- Direct inquiry channels: 0–10.
- Operational complexity: 0–10.
- Reservations team evidence: 0–10.
- Active business signals: 0–5.
- Decision-maker accessibility: 0–10.
- Technology gap: 0–10.

## Audit score: maximum 20 points
- First response speed: 0–6.
- Answer quality: 0–5.
- Qualification quality: 0–3.
- Handoff/ownership: 0–3.
- Follow-up behaviour: 0–3.

## 90+ threshold
A company can score above 90 only after real audit evidence exists. Do not assign invented scores where data is missing.

`lead_list.csv` is a research seed list with 68 named companies. It is not a verified sales pipeline and not a list of 90+ prospects.
