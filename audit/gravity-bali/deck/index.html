The leaks

Where context and follow-up can disappear

  • Channel switch: website → WhatsApp likely drops villa, dates and source — the chat restarts from “Hi, is the villa available?” Inferred link behavior not tested
  • Five inboxes: form-mail, WhatsApp, Instagram DM, OTA threads, email — no unified pipeline visible from outside. Inferred
  • No visible follow-up system: quiet threads depend on memory; no reminders or recovery evidence. Inferred
  • Funnel invisible: sources, response times and lost-reasons aren’t recorded anywhere we can see — so “why didn’t they book?” has no answer today. Inferred

These are process observations, not people observations — your team’s service reputation is exactly why the plumbing deserves to match it.

Concept — first message that keeps context

“Hi! I’m interested in Villa Kelanah, 12–16 Aug, 6 guests.” (arrives pre-filled from the villa page, source recorded)

“Wonderful choice! Checking Kelanah for those dates now — any little ones joining?” (instant, 24/7; your concierge takes over with full context)

The system

Villa Ops OS — what it is (and isn’t)

Built for you in 14 days

  • AI-assisted WhatsApp first responder in Gravity’s tone — instant ack, FAQs, 24/7 incl. weekends
  • Qualification: dates · party · budget · villa · requests
  • Handoff rules → your concierges, instantly notified
  • Villa-page → WhatsApp deep links (context carried)
  • One pipeline: form + WA + IG + email · owners · statuses
  • Follow-up reminders · abandoned-enquiry recovery
  • Dashboard: median response time, sources, conversion
  • Shadow mode first · training · runbook · 30-day support

Explicitly not

  • Not a PMS / channel-manager replacement
  • Not ads, SEO or revenue management
  • Not a human call centre; never replaces your concierges
  • No revenue guarantees — results depend on availability, pricing, season
  • No integration claimed until validated on your systems (Days 1–2)

Shadow mode = Days 11–12: the system drafts, your team approves; no guest sees an unreviewed reply before launch.

▶ Try it yourself: interactive demo on your scenarios (Saturday-night enquiry, quiet guest, wedding proposal) — /audit/gravity-bali/demo/. You play the guest; the team side reacts live. Simulated data.
