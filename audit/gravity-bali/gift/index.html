01

A WhatsApp Business greeting that answers the Saturday-night guest

≈ 2 minutes · WhatsApp Business app · free

Right now a guest messaging about a villa at 22:41 on a Saturday gets silence until Monday. WhatsApp Business has a built-in Greeting message (first-ever contact) and Away message (outside your hours) — set once, sent automatically. Here’s text in your tone, ready to paste:

Greeting / away message
Hello, and thank you for reaching out to Gravity Bali 🌴

We’ve received your message and your enquiry matters to us. Our reservations team personally prepares every quote, and they’re currently offline — you’ll have a reply by 9:00 AM Bali time.

To help us come back to you faster, feel free to share:
• Which villa you’re interested in
• Your dates
• Number of guests

Talk very soon,
The Gravity Bali team
How to set it:
  1. WhatsApp Business app → Settings → Business tools → Away message
  2. Turn it on → paste the text above → schedule “Outside business hours” (set your hours)
  3. Repeat for Greeting message (sent to anyone messaging for the first time)

Honest limit: this is a fixed auto-reply — it can’t read the villa/dates, can’t answer questions, and doesn’t wake anyone up. It stops the silence; it doesn’t handle the conversation. That’s the gap the full system closes.

02

Fix the “7 days a week” vs “closed on weekends” contradiction

≈ 5 minutes · your website CMS · free

Your Conciergerie page says the concierge runs 7 days a week; your guest FAQ says it’s closed on weekends. A Saturday guest can’t tell which is true. Pick one honest version and use it on both pages:

Option A — if someone really does watch WhatsApp on weekends
Our guest concierge is available every day, including weekends. Messages sent outside office hours are acknowledged straight away and answered by our on-duty team as quickly as possible — with urgent matters always prioritised.
Option B — if weekends are genuinely quieter (honest and still reassuring)
Our concierge team is in the office Monday to Friday. On weekends we still watch WhatsApp for anything urgent — you’ll always receive an acknowledgement, and your dedicated villa manager is reachable for time-sensitive needs during your stay.

How: open both pages in your website editor (Conciergerie + Guest FAQ), replace the conflicting lines with the same chosen paragraph, save. Done.

03

A WhatsApp button that carries the villa & dates into the chat

≈ 15 minutes · your web developer · free

Today, a guest who switches from a villa page to WhatsApp starts from “Hi, is the villa available?” — your team has to re-ask everything. This button pre-fills the villa name so the chat starts with context. Drop it on each villa page (change the villa name per page):

<a class="wa-enquire"
   href="https://wa.me/6281353704430?text=Hi%20Gravity%20Bali!%20I%27m%20interested%20in%20Villa%20Kelanah.%20My%20dates%3A%20___%20to%20___%2C%20for%20___%20guests.%20(via%20website)"
   target="_blank" rel="noopener">
  Enquire about Villa Kelanah on WhatsApp
</a>
How & notes for your developer:
  1. Replace 6281353704430 with your WhatsApp number in international format (no +, no spaces) — confirm it’s the reservations line.
  2. Change Villa%20Kelanah to each page’s villa (spaces = %20).
  3. Optional: if your villa pages already have a date picker, have the developer inject the chosen dates into the text= parameter dynamically.

Honest limit: this carries context into the first message, but nobody records it, assigns an owner, or follows up — that still happens by hand across your inboxes. Capturing, routing and following up every enquiry is the paid system’s job.
