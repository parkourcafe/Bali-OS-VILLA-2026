# Sales Call System — Selena Systems GTM v1.0

Goal: close scoped Pilot, Core, Growth, or Enterprise implementations for Villa Ops Response System. Tone: calm diagnostician, not seller.

## 1. 15-minute qualification call
- 0–2 min: Frame.
- 2–8 min: Three qualifiers:
  1. Portfolio: "How many villas do you manage, and are they premium or standard?"
  2. Inquiry volume: "Roughly how many direct inquiries land per month across WhatsApp, website, Instagram, email, and direct booking?"
  3. Handling: "Who answers, and what happens after hours?"
- 8–12 min: Use mystery-shop wording. One test is a signal, not proof.
- 12–15 min: Qualified -> book discovery. Not qualified -> smaller pilot, park, or referral.

Disqualifiers: below ICP without pilot fit; no decision-maker access; no meaningful direct inquiry flow; advanced internal AI response system already in place; wants revenue guarantees; refuses tool/API costs; unresolved legal/contracting path.

## 2. 45-minute discovery call
- 0–5: Agenda.
- 5–20: Walk one inquiry end-to-end.
- 20–30: ROI estimate using retained revenue.
- 30–38: Demo + scope.
- 38–45: Price, terms, close.

## 3. Diagnosis framework: the 5 leaks
1. Speed.
2. Coverage.
3. Follow-up.
4. Ownership.
5. Visibility.

Score 6+ -> strong fit, but do not assign invented lead scores without evidence.

## 4. ROI calculation framework (estimate)
For management companies:

Recovered management revenue = recovered bookings x average booking value x management commission percentage.

Optional estimate lines: direct-booking commission saved, staff hours saved, reduced leakage from missed follow-up.

Never use total guest booking value unless the client owns the property and retains that revenue. Never claim one booking automatically pays for the system unless actual retained revenue supports that statement.

## 5. Objection handling
- **"Too expensive."** Compare to estimated retained revenue leakage and staff load, not total booking value.
- **"We have auto-replies / a channel manager."** Auto-replies acknowledge; this system answers, qualifies, follows up, and hands off.
- **"AI will embarrass us."** Approved knowledge base, mandatory human review, shadow mode, acceptance tests.
- **"Market is bad."** This is leak repair for inquiries already earned.
- **"No case studies?"** Correct. Demo, audit signal, and founding-client terms replace invented proof.
- **"Can we start smaller?"** Pilot: $1,500–2,500 with reduced scope.

## 6. Closing script
"Here is how starting looks: 50% today holds the kickoff slot for {date}. Days one and two we lock scope, confirm access, and approve the delivery path. The 14-day timeline applies only if WhatsApp/API access is available or not on the critical path, we start with maximum two channels, one availability source, no custom PMS integration, and your team approves the knowledge base on time. At go-live, we test that 95% of eligible new inquiries on covered channels receive a first automated response within two minutes, excluding outages, spam, unsupported channels, out-of-scope messages, mandatory human-review cases, and client-side access issues. Shall I send the agreement and deposit invoice today?"

## 7. Follow-up email after call
Subject: {Company} — scope, estimate, and next step

Hi {Name}, thank you for the time today. What we established: [3 bullets — inquiry flow, estimate, scope]. The ROI estimate uses retained management revenue, not total booking value. To hold {date} kickoff: signed agreement + 50% deposit. Open items: local contracting entity, invoice issuer, payment recipient, data-processing responsibility, IP ownership, and any legal/accounting confirmation needed.

## 8. Payment terms
Use 50% upfront / 50% at go-live for clients 1–5. Never start at 0% down.
