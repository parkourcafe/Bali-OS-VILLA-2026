## 2. Pain
**Header:** Where bookings quietly leak

An inquiry arrives at 11pm. The guest is comparing multiple villas. If the answer waits until morning, the guest may already be gone. In a market where occupancy is under pressure and rates are discounted, the inquiry you already earned matters more.

## 3. Solution
**Header:** A response system, not a chatbot

We install an AI layer over the channels you already use. It answers from your approved information only, qualifies serious inquiries, follows up with quiet conversations, and routes complaints, emergencies, refund requests, sensitive personal information, and unusual requests to a human.

## 4. Main offer
**Header:** Villa Ops Response System

One scoped implementation for qualified Bali villa management companies. Built around WhatsApp, website, and optionally Instagram or email, depending on scope. You own the accounts, documentation, and handover.

## 5. Who it is for
Best fit: Bali villa management company or multi-property rental operator; ideally 20–60 managed villas; minimum 15 premium villas or approximately 30 standard villas; at least 2 people handling reservations; preferably 150–300+ direct inquiry conversations per month; premium or international segment; accessible owner/GM/operations/reservations lead; no obvious advanced internal AI response system.

Smaller operators may qualify for a reduced-scope pilot.

## 6. Commercial promise
95% of eligible new inquiries on covered channels receive a first automated response within two minutes.

Exclusions: Meta, BSP, PMS, hosting, internet, or third-party outages; spam and duplicate messages; unsupported channels; messages outside agreed automation scope; messages requiring mandatory human review; client-side access or configuration failures.

## 7. Deliverables
AI response agent on scoped channels; booking-inquiry qualification; approved guest FAQ automation; follow-up for silent inquiries; unified inbox/pipeline; response-time dashboard; escalation rules; documentation; team training; 14 days post-launch tuning.
