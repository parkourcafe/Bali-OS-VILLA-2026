# Assumptions Log — Selena Systems GTM v1.0

Every item below is an ASSUMPTION, not a verified fact. Verify before relying on it in sales conversations.

## About Selena Systems
- A1. Selena Systems is a solo-founder or very small team with hands-on AI implementation skill.
- A2. Selena Systems has no public case studies yet. All proof strategy assumes zero existing testimonials. If proof exists, upgrade the assets.
- A3. The founder can take calls in Bali (WITA, UTC+8) and meet Bali clients in person.
- A4. The founder has access to a local partner capable of supporting legal local sales and contracting. This does not resolve the legal setup by itself.

## Local partner operational checklist
Unresolved before signing any local Indonesian-entity client:
- Identify the contracting entity.
- Identify who issues the invoice.
- Identify who receives payment.
- Define the local partner's fee or revenue share.
- Define responsibility for client delivery.
- Define data-processing responsibility.
- Define intellectual-property ownership.
- Obtain local legal/accounting confirmation before signing.

Do not state that Bali sales are automatically blocked. Also do not invent legal conclusions. Treat this as an operational/legal gating checklist.
