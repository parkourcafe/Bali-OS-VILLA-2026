# Outreach Scripts — Selena Systems GTM v1.0

Rules baked in: calm, specific, evidence-led, no hype, no fake urgency, no mass-blasting. Every message references something real and public about the prospect. A single test is a signal, not proof of systemic failure.

Placeholders: {Name} {Company} {Specific} {X} {date} {channel}.

Required audit wording:
"In one test conducted on {date}, the response took {X}. This does not establish your normal performance, but it identified a scenario worth reviewing."

## 10. Loom/video audit script
- 0:00–0:20 — "Hi {Name}, {Founder} from Selena Systems in Bali. Three minutes: what I tested, what it may mean, what I would automate."
- 0:20–1:00 — Screen: the single inquiry and timestamps. Use required wording. Do not claim systemic failure.
- 1:00–1:50 — Estimate: use their inputs where known. For managers, use retained management revenue, not total booking value.
- 1:50–2:40 — The fix: Villa Ops Response System. First automated response within two minutes for eligible covered inquiries; approved knowledge base; human review for sensitive messages; 40-scenario acceptance suite before go-live.
- 2:40–3:00 — CTA: "If this is worth 15 minutes, grab a slot here {link} or reply here."

## Channel sequencing
Bali leads: audit signal -> WhatsApp or in-person -> follow-up 1 -> Loom audit -> follow-up 2. Remote leads: audit signal -> email -> LinkedIn -> Loom audit -> follow-ups. Max 4 touches, then park for 60 days.
