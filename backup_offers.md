# Backup Offers — Selena Systems GTM v1.0

Both reuse parts of the Villa Ops Response System technical core. Switch positioning and guardrails, not the operating discipline.

## Backup #1 — AI Lead Response & Follow-Up for Real Estate Agencies
- **Target:** Bali agencies actively transacting; wave 2: Dubai brokerages.
- **Pain:** slow buyer response, long nurture cycles, shared WhatsApp leakage.
- **Promise:** eligible buyer inquiries on covered channels receive a first automated response within two minutes, then qualification and routing.
- **Deliverables:** AI responder, qualification script, calendar booking, nurture sequence, pipeline board, dashboard, handover + training.
- **Price:** Core $5,000; Growth $7,500–9,500; Enterprise from $12,000.
- **ROI logic:** estimate recovered retained commission only. One recovered deal may justify the system, but never claim it as guaranteed.
- **Kill criteria:** no fresh listings, no decision-maker access, no permission to handle buyer data correctly.

## Backup #2 — AI Patient Inquiry & Booking System for Aesthetic/Wellness Clinics
- **Target:** Bali clinics serving international clients.
- **Pain:** missed inquiries during treatments, after-hours message decay, no-show risk.
- **Promise:** covered inquiries receive fast approved information and consultation routing.
- **Hard guardrails:** no medical advice, diagnosis, suitability claims, outcome promises, or treatment recommendations. Clinical questions route to staff.
- **Price:** $5,000–7,500.
- **ROI logic:** estimated recovered consultations and no-show reduction only.
- **Kill criteria:** clinic wants AI to discuss medical suitability or outcomes.

## Separate project type — Cold-Lead Revival Sprint
Use only when database quality, permission basis, opt-out handling, and sending limits are confirmed. Do not frame a $5,000 revival sprint as a small down-sell from a $6,500 core package. Price and scope should be quoted separately based on list size, compliance risk, and expected manual review.
