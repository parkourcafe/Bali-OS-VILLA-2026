## Why we studied Gravity Bali

We audit direct-booking journeys of Bali's best villa operators. Gravity Bali
stood out for the opposite of the usual reason: your service promises are the
strongest we found — a concierge answering "in under five minutes", booking
requests "under 1 hour", direct-booking perks OTAs can't match. Companies that
promise this well deserve plumbing that makes the promise easy to keep. From
the outside, we believe yours doesn't yet — and that the gap is closable in
14 days.

## What we found (summary — full audit on your private page)

1. **Your best-perks channel is your slowest.** Direct booking earns guests
   the best price, breakfast and welcome tray — but runs through a form "sent
   directly to our marketing team" for a "personalized quote", while the same
   villas confirm instantly on OTAs. *(Observed on your public pages.)*
2. **The speed promise has no visible meter.** "<5 minutes average" is
   published on two pages; nothing public suggests response times are
   measured. We did NOT test actual speed — that requires your data, and we
   make no claim it is slow.
3. **Coverage contradicts itself.** "7 days a week" (Conciergerie page) vs
   "the concierge service is closed on weekends" (guest FAQ). Weekend
   enquirers can't know what to expect.
4. **Context likely dies at the channel switch.** No visible mechanism carries
   villa/dates/source from a villa page into WhatsApp. *(Inferred; 2-minute
   joint check on the walkthrough.)*
5. **Five inboxes, no single pipeline visible.** Form-mail, WhatsApp,
   Instagram, OTA threads, email — with no unified view, follow-up and source
   attribution depend on memory. *(Inferred.)*

None of this is a criticism of your team — the observations are about process
plumbing, and your reputation is exactly why it deserves upgrading.
