## 2. What we will build
1. AI response agent on covered channels, normally WhatsApp Business API and website chat, with Instagram/email only if scoped.
2. Inquiry qualification for dates, guests, budget, purpose, and urgency.
3. Automated follow-up of silent inquiries.
4. Guest FAQ automation from the approved knowledge base.
5. Human escalation for complaints, emergencies, refund requests, sensitive personal information, unusual requests, and mandatory-review topics.
6. Unified conversation pipeline with ownership and status.
7. Response-time dashboard.
8. Handover: documentation, team training, and 14 days post-launch support.

## 3. Timeline
Kickoff {date} -> scope/access lock by day 2 -> core agent and knowledge base -> follow-up and pipeline -> 40-scenario acceptance suite -> training -> shadow go-live.

The 14-day timeline applies only when WhatsApp/API access is available or not on the critical path, maximum two initial channels are included, one availability source is used, no custom PMS integration is required, the knowledge base is approved on time, and rollout starts in shadow mode.

## 4. Acceptance criteria
- 95% of eligible new inquiries on covered channels receive a first automated response within two minutes.
- Exclusions: Meta, BSP, PMS, hosting, internet, or third-party outages; spam and duplicate messages; unsupported channels; out-of-scope messages; mandatory human-review messages; client-side access or configuration failures.
- 40 required test scenarios completed: 20 historical inquiries, 10 adversarial scenarios, 10 operational/failure-mode scenarios.
- Team trained; documentation delivered; account ownership confirmed.
