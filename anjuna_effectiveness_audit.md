# Effectiveness Audit — Anjuna Bay / Balinest Villas (Uluwatu)

Applied `competitor_audit_method.md` to the Anjuna rental operation. **Public data only.** Every OTA
and social page returned HTTP 403 to automated fetching, so figures come from search-index snippets
and third-party analytics (airbtics, airroi). Confidence flagged throughout; estimates labelled.

## Key structural finding
Two entities — do not conflate:
- **The Anjuna Bay** (theanjunabay.com, IG @anjunabay) = real-estate **developer / sales** ("Luxury Real Estate"). Not a booking channel.
- **Balinest Villas** (balinestvillas.com) = the **rental manager** running Anjuna's guest stays via **Guesty**, syndicated to OTAs. **~207 listings managed, 4.9★, ~85% occupancy, +285% portfolio growth in 12 months** (airbtics). **This is the real ICP target.**

## Verdict
| Dimension | Rating | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Demand / occupancy | **HIGH** | Guest-favorite + Superhost badges, 44–86 reviews on new units, ~85% claimed occupancy, 7+ OTA channels incl. Marriott H&V |
| Reputation | **HIGH** | 4.87–5.0★ Airbnb, 8.7/10 Booking with Staff 9.5; only structural gripes (noise, construction) |
| Responsiveness gap | **MEDIUM (leak, low-confidence)** | On-ground service praised, but digital first-response unconfirmed and a 200-listing book scaling +285%/yr is where latency breaks |
| Marketing | **MEDIUM-LOW** | Bookings ride OTA algorithms; owned brand sells real estate not stays; no confirmed rental ads, no Google review presence, no direct-booking demand engine |

**Bottom line.** A well-distributed, well-reviewed, high-occupancy operation winning on **OTA reach +
genuinely good on-ground service + aggressive pricing** — not on owned marketing. The "Anjuna Bay"
brand targets property buyers; guest bookings quietly run through **Balinest's Guesty backend across a
fast-growing ~200-listing portfolio**. The **biggest leak is guest communication at scale**: inquiries
now land across Airbnb, Booking, Agoda, Trip.com, Marriott and the direct Guesty site at once, and a
lean team growing ~4×/yr cannot hold fast, 24/7, multilingual first-response across all of them — the
exact pre-booking + check-in moment where bookings and review scores are won or lost. **An AI
guest-inquiry/response layer that unifies every channel and answers instantly** plugs the one weakness
in an otherwise strong operation.

## Action
- **Add Balinest Villas as a prospect** (the real manager). Ops-complexity fit is strong; size (~207) is above the 20–60 sweet spot → strategic/enterprise framing, like BaliSuperHost.
- Anjuna Bay stays as a developer/upsell row.
- Outreach hook for Balinest: "You run ~200 listings across 6+ channels at 4.9★ — the one place that breaks at 4×/yr growth is 24/7 multilingual first-response across all those inboxes."
