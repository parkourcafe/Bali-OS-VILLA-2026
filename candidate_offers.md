Format per offer: target / core pain / promise / deliverables / price / why they pay / lead source / sales angle / risks / proof needed / complexity / ROI logic. Source counts are limited to the current package: 52 pain signals, 12 markets, 12 candidate offers, source groups S1–S49, and 15 assumptions.

## O1. Villa Ops Response System — Bali villa management companies ⭐
- Target: Bali villa management company or multi-property rental operator; ideally 20–60 managed villas; minimum 15 premium villas or approximately 30 standard villas.
- Pain: night inquiries from AU/EU time zones; WhatsApp/channel chaos; slow or inconsistent follow-up; OTA response-rate pressure (S14, S17, S22).
- Promise: 95% of eligible new inquiries on covered channels receive a first automated response within two minutes.
- Deliverables: AI response agent on scoped channels; booking-inquiry qualification; availability/FAQ answers from approved knowledge base; human escalation for sensitive or out-of-scope messages; unified inbox/pipeline; response dashboard; handover docs, team training, and 14 days support.
- Price: Pilot $1,500–2,500; Core $5,000; Growth $7,500–9,500; Enterprise from $12,000.
- Why they pay: occupancy and discount pressure make inquiry leakage expensive; direct inquiry handling affects retained management revenue and team workload.
- Leads: `lead_list.csv` research seed list, villa-management directories, OTA listings, Google Maps, competitor "best of" lists.
- Angle: single mystery-shop audit as a signal: "In one test conducted on {date}, the response took {X}. This does not establish your normal performance, but it identified a scenario worth reviewing."
- Risks: some operators already have channel managers or internal response systems; legal/local contracting details unresolved; smaller operators may only fit a pilot.
- Proof needed: live demo agent; before/after response-time log; acceptance test suite, not invented case studies.
- Complexity: medium. WhatsApp/API provisioning and PMS access can move timeline if not available.
- ROI: estimate retained management revenue, not total guest booking value: recovered bookings x average booking value x management commission percentage, plus estimated staff hours saved, direct-booking commission saved, and reduced follow-up leakage where applicable.

## O2. AI Lead Response & Follow-Up System — real estate agencies (Bali)
- Target: Bali agencies actively transacting with international buyers.
- Pain: slow lead response (S4); long buyer nurture; shared WhatsApp leakage.
- Promise: eligible buyer inquiries answered within two minutes where scoped, qualified, routed, and followed up.
- Deliverables: AI responder on WhatsApp/web forms/IG; qualification script; calendar booking; nurture sequence; pipeline board; handover + training.
- Price: $5,000 core; $7,500–9,500 growth; enterprise from $12,000.
- ROI: estimate retained commission from recovered deals; never promise a deal.

## O3. AI Patient Inquiry & Booking System — aesthetic/wellness clinics (Bali)
- Target: clinics serving international medical tourists.

- Promise: scoped inquiries answered quickly with approved treatment information and consultation routing.
- Guardrail: no diagnosis, medical advice, outcome promises, or suitability assessment.
- Price: $5,000–7,500. Backup, not primary.
