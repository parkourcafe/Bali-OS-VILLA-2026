## Where Anjuna stands in the pack (rating comparison)
- **Airbnb: top-quartile.** 4.87–5.0 matches/beats Zyloh (4.89) and area average; **B1's 86 reviews out-review most new-build rivals** (Ayora ~9, Marghe 4). No walkable-to-Thomas-Beach rival has Airbnb review depth yet → **B1's review moat is winnable ground**.
- **Booking: below the visible wall.** Same-district rivals render "Exceptional/Superb" (COZ 9.3–9.6, Hakuna 9.1/240, Mule Malu 9.3/694, Ayora 9.8) while Anjuna's **8.7/59 renders "Fabulous"** — and Booking guests filter/sort on exactly that. **Weak flank = the Booking score; strongest lever = fixing Value/WiFi subscores + expectation management.**
- **Price flank:** Marghe $136 / Ayora $117 undercut from below with free-cancellation titles; Anjuna's $144 non-refundable sits in between with a worse cancellation story.

## Caveats
Funnel A (Airbnb per-listing ratings) is least externally indexable — thinnest numbers; verify in
the manual browser sweep. Ayora/Marghe scores ride tiny review counts (9 and 4) — high but fragile.
Bukit Vista unit-level data not indexed — flagged, not padded.
