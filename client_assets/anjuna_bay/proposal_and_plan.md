# Anjuna Bay — Owner Proposal, 14-Day Plan, KPIs & Limitations

## Proposal (to the Anjuna Bay owner side)

**What we observed (public data only).** Your villas earn 4.87–5.0★ and fill well — the on-ground
team is doing real work. But public numbers suggest the results are smaller than the asset: nightly
rates sit under the Uluwatu median ($144 "from" vs ~$216 median), demand is almost entirely
OTA-sourced (with OTA commissions on nearly every booking), the only marketing brand targets
property buyers rather than guests, and first-response speed across the 6+ booking channels is
unverified. High occupancy at a low rate is not the same as maximum owner revenue.

**What we propose.** A 14-day install of the **Villa Ops Response System** on the Anjuna rental
flow — one governed response layer over every inquiry channel:
- approved first reply within 2 minutes on covered channels, 24/7, multilingual;
- structured qualification (dates, party, budget, purpose, add-ons);
- D1/D3/D7 follow-up on quiet leads; escalation to humans for VIP/pricing/complaints;
- a weekly owner report: inquiries → response times → conversions → ADR vs market.

Alongside (recommendations, executed by you/your operator, made measurable by our reporting):
a guarded reprice toward market median, and a rental-brand presence (Google profile, guest-facing
IG) so demand stops being 100% OTA-dependent.

**What this is not.** Not a chatbot replacing your team; not a revenue guarantee; not a PMS
replacement — it sits on top of Guesty and the existing channels.

**First step (free).** A Secret Guest Audit: one respectful test inquiry through your public flow,
timed and scored (auto-reply vs human, qualification, follow-up, tone). Report in 48h.

## KPIs (what we commit to measure)
- ≥95% of eligible inquiries on covered channels get an approved first response **< 2 minutes**.
- 100% of inquiries logged with qualification fields (dates/party/budget/purpose).
- Follow-up sent D1/D3/D7 to every quiet lead.
- Escalation to a human < 15 minutes for flagged cases (complaint/VIP/pricing exception).
- Weekly owner report delivered every Monday.

## Limitations (honest)
- We do **not** guarantee occupancy, revenue, ADR, or review scores — pricing and hospitality
  decisions remain with the owner/operator; we make them fast, consistent and visible.
- Public-data findings are search-index-based (sites block robots); the Secret Guest Audit is one
  test — a signal, not proof of systemic performance.
- Seasonality, on-site construction, nightclub noise and OTA algorithm changes are outside our control.
- OTA message channels vary in API access; coverage per channel is confirmed in days 3–5.
