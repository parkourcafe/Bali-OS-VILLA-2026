Maps the 6-stage guest booking journey (client framework) onto what our audit already covers, what's
a gap, and how each competitor "wins" at each stage. Confirms the comp set is the Stage 3-4 layer and
gives the scorecard its real-world weights (Stage 4 decision factors).

## Booking windows → outreach/marketing timing (from framework)
AU 30-90d · EU 60-180d · US 45-120d · SG 7-30d · ID 3-21d · honeymooners 90-180d · families 60-150d ·
surfers 14-60d. Implication for the new operator: Anjuna's surf+cliff product skews AU/EU/surfer →
**must be visible 2-6 months out** (Inspiration stage), which is exactly where it currently has no
owned brand. Reinforces audit move #3 (build the guest-facing brand).
