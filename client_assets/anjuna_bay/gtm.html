Positioning, Pricing & Go-To-Market

Positioning
"Brand-new cliffside pool villas, a short walk from Thomas Beach — the view, the private pool and the privacy people come to Uluwatu for, without the Canggu crowds."
For: couples/honeymooners + surfers first, then luxury & nomads. Why it wins: travelers' top-3 loves are exactly cliff/sunset view, private pool, quiet privacy — Anjuna has all three but sells unit codes. Guardrail: "next Canggu" sentiment + on-site construction is real — lean into quiet/private/walk-to-beach and disclose construction honestly.

The plan, step by step

1

Own website + Google Yes

Anjuna's direct booking is the weakest in the whole comp set (Guesty, 1 unit indexed) while COZ, NOUR, Zyloh, Young Villas, Uluwatu Surf all run own sites. Direct saves 15–18% commission + builds a guest database. You already have Guesty — it's design+config, not new tech.

  • Fast mobile site, real-time availability, one page per villa, WhatsApp button (guests expect it), book-direct incentive (best rate + free airport transfer — answers the #1 complaint).
  • Google Business Profile — create it (missing). Free; puts villas on Maps + the "villas in Uluwatu" map-pack.
  • Google Vacation Rentals / free booking links — direct site shows commission-free next to OTAs.
  • Google Ads: yes but small, after the profile+site — (a) brand-defense on "Anjuna Bay villa"; (b) a tight "Uluwatu private pool villa" search campaign, geo AU/EU → direct site.
2

Own advertising — paid social Yes

The Inspiration stage (30–180 days out) where Anjuna has zero presence. Small budget, tight targeting.

  • Creative = the top-3 loves: cliff/sunset view, private pool, walk to Thomas Beach — sunset pool reels beat any text.
  • Targeting: Australia first (dominant origin), then EU couples/honeymooners + surfers; retarget site/IG visitors.
  • Timing: weight spend 2–6 months before AU/EU high season (their booking windows).
  • Split the guest brand from @anjunabay (which sells real estate) — guests ≠ buyers.
3

SEO Yes — paired with ads

  • Local SEO first (fastest, missing): Google Business Profile + reviews flow — where "villa Uluwatu" map searches convert.
  • On-site: one indexable page per villa with landmark benefit titles ("2BR Cliffside Pool Villa, 5-min walk to Thomas Beach"); fix the 5-page thin site + title typo.
  • Content (medium-term): blog owning long-tail — "where to stay in Uluwatu for surfers", "honeymoon villa Uluwatu near Padang Padang" (how Uluwatu Surf Villas / Cabo win research-first guests).
  • SEO is slow → Google Business + ads bridge the gap now.
4

Links

  • Link-in-bio (Linktree): IG/TikTok → booking engine + WhatsApp + Google reviews (every strong rival has one; Anjuna none).
  • Consistent listing links/naming across OTAs — same landmark name + photos everywhere (fixes B10-only-Booking, B3-only-Agoda fragmentation).
  • Earned/backlinks: get into "best villas in Uluwatu/Bingin" roundups + surf-travel resellers (Perfect Wave, The Perfect Hideaway); partner links — Canna Beach Club, surf schools, wedding planners.
  • Google Business + review links on the site and in post-stay follow-ups.

What to improve — priority order

#1List ALL completed inventory on every major channel; onboard Palms on handover — multiplies booking count (the top lever).
#2Rename every listing to landmark benefit titles; kill unit codes; fix the typo + Agoda mismatch.
#3Reprice + free-cancel + parity + dynamic pricing — captures the ~24% rate gap.
#4Google Business Profile + own site + WhatsApp + book-direct incentive — closes the weakest-in-set direct-booking gap.
#5Consolidate review equity + start a Google review flow — lifts the 8.7 Booking flank, transfers proof to new units.
#6Manage construction/noise in copy (honest disclosure + mitigation) — protects the 4.9★ through 2026.
#7Add missing channels — Vrbo/Expedia, Traveloka, TripAdvisor, metasearch, luxury agencies, surf resellers.
#82-minute response layer + weekly owner report — the pre-arrival WhatsApp guests expect + the owner visibility that's missing.

Keep (don't touch): the on-ground service (Booking Staff 9.5, welcome-WhatsApp), the Guesty stack, and the top-tier review ratings. Change the commercial wrapper, not the hospitality.
