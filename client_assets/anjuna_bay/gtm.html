Positioning
"Brand-new cliffside pool villas, a short walk from Thomas Beach — the view, the private pool and the privacy people come to Uluwatu for, without the Canggu crowds."
For: couples/honeymooners + premium surf travelers, then luxury. Guardrail: construction & nightlife are audible and documented — disclosure ships Week 1, before any price move, and inside ad creative. Selling "quiet privacy" over rock-breaker noise manufactures the market's "photos ≠ reality" complaint.

The plan, step by step

1

Own website + Google — corrected mechanics

  • New direct site (brand-licensed): Guesty engine, page per villa, WhatsApp button. Direct economics stated net: after ~3% processing + perk + acquisition, year-one advantage ≈ 5–10% — the bigger prize is the guest database, repeat stays and parity leverage.
  • Parity — compliant: public rates at parity everywhere (Booking/Agoda clauses bind in Indonesia); direct wins via closed member rates + perks + repeat codes. Week 1: remove the "book on Airbnb for best rates" message.
  • Google Business Profile — conditional: unstaffed vacation rentals are ineligible; verify a staffed reception first. Otherwise the map path is the Google Vacation Rentals feed via Guesty (engine live + 8 photos/listing, ~3-week approval) — incremental upside, not a pillar.
  • Google Ads — capped & gated: brand = exact match [anjuna bay villa]/[anjuna bay uluwatu], negatives goa/india/anjunabeats/anjunadeep, geo-exclude India, cap $100–200/mo (anti-conquesting only). Generic "uluwatu private pool villa" launches only after tracking is live.

Weeks 1–2: GA4 + Guesty purchase event · Meta pixel + CAPI · UTM schema · WhatsApp click tracking · attribution sheet. No ad spend before this exists. Retargeting staged for month 3+ (audience ≈ zero until then — prospecting carries the plan).

  • Links: Linktree hub; consistent naming/photos across OTAs; roundups + surf resellers (Perfect Wave) + partners (Canna, surf schools, wedding planners).

Rollout rules (no 2× jump):
  1. Disclosure first (Week 1) — honest construction/nightlife copy before any increase.
  2. Stepped: +15–20% per 4–6-week cycle, watched on 30-day pickup pace.
  3. Season-gated: peak stay-dates first; shoulder slower. Unit-gated: quiet units first; noise-exposed units keep a disclosed "construction rate".
  4. Grandfather existing bookings; new rates on stay-dates 60+ days out; scripted reservations answer.
  5. Auto-rollback: pickup pace −15% vs LY pauses the ladder. Free-cancellation rate added alongside non-ref.
  6. Dynamic pricing (~$20/listing/mo). Lago Villas "92%/+48%" = vendor claim, unverified — same skepticism as Balinest's 85%. Verified support: top-quartile sustains $313+ here.
