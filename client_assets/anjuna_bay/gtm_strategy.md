## 1. Positioning
**For:** couples/honeymooners + premium surf travelers first; luxury next.
**Promise:** *"Brand-new cliffside pool villas, a short walk from Thomas Beach — the view, the private
pool and the privacy people come to Uluwatu for, without the Canggu crowds."*
**Why:** travelers' top-3 loves = cliff/sunset view, private pool, quiet privacy — Anjuna has all
three and currently sells unit codes.
**Honesty guardrail:** phase-2 construction and the neighboring club are audible and documented in
reviews. Disclosure + mitigation copy ships in Week 1, **before** any price move and inside ad
creative — selling "quiet privacy" reels over rock-breaker noise manufactures the market's
"photos ≠ reality" complaint and endangers the 8.7 Booking score.

## 2. Own website + Google — yes, with corrected mechanics
- **Direct site (new, owner-brand-licensed):** fast, mobile-first, Guesty booking engine, one page per
  villa, WhatsApp button (the Uluwatu norm). Direct is justified **net**, honestly: after ~3% payment
  processing + the welcome perk + amortized acquisition, year-one net advantage is ~5–10% (not the
  gross 15–18% commission) — the bigger value is the **guest database, repeat/long-stay bookings and
  parity leverage**.
- **Rate parity — compliant version.** Public rates stay at parity across channels (Booking/Agoda
  parity clauses still bind in Indonesia). Direct advantage via **closed member rates** (behind
  email/WhatsApp signup), **perks** (airport transfer, floating breakfast, late checkout) and
  unpublished repeat-guest codes. Week-1 action: remove the operator's current "book on Airbnb for
  best rates" message.
- **Google Business Profile — conditional, not assumed.** Google excludes unstaffed vacation rentals
  from GBP. Eligible **only if** a staffed on-site reception/office with posted hours exists — verify
  first. If ineligible, the map-pack path is the **Google Vacation Rentals feed via Guesty**
  (requires the booking engine live + 8+ photos/listing; ~3 weeks approval) — treated as incremental
  upside, not a pillar; review flow then targets OTA reviews, not Google.
- **Google Ads — small, capped, gated on tracking.**
  (a) *Brand:* exact match only `[anjuna bay villa]`, `[anjuna bay uluwatu]`; negatives: goa, india,
  north goa, anjunabeats, anjunadeep; geo-exclude India; cap **$100–200/mo**; purpose = anti-OTA-
  conquesting only (organic brand volume is near zero — there is little to "defend").
  (b) *Generic:* tight "uluwatu private pool villa / villa near thomas beach" exact/phrase, geo
  AU→UK/DE/FR→US, **launched only after the tracking stack is live**.

## 9. Pricing — one rate card, stepped, gated (fully rewritten)
**Verified baseline:** 2BR **$144** non-refundable (public). 1BR baseline **unverified** (single
low-confidence $66 Agoda snippet — likely promo/pre-tax); real rates come from the Week-0 Guesty pull.
**Market (AirROI 2026, one sourced set):** market avg ADR **$267** · median listing **$216** ·
2–3BR ADR **~$259** · RevPAR **$130** · top-25% **$313+**.

**Target ladder (single source of truth — the segment table references it, never restates):**
- 1BR → **$110–140** (subject to verified baseline)
- 2BR → **$190–240 base**, **$280+ peak**
- 3BR → **$280–350**
- $300+ is a **milestone**, unlocked after Booking score >9.0 and construction end — not a launch price.

**How it's rolled out (revenue-manager sequencing, not a 2× jump):**
1. **Disclosure first** (Week 1): honest construction/nightlife copy + mitigation before any increase.
2. **Stepped:** +15–20% per 4–6-week cycle, monitored on 30-day pickup pace and conversion.
3. **Season-gated:** new rates land on peak stay-dates (Jun–Aug, holidays) first; shoulder moves slower.
4. **Unit-gated:** quiet/far-from-construction units reprice first; noise-exposed units keep a
   disclosed "construction rate" discount until phase 2 ends.
5. **Grandfathering:** existing reservations honored; new rates apply to stay-dates 60+ days out
   first; scripted reservations response for rate-difference questions.
6. **Auto-rollback:** if pickup pace drops >15% vs prior year at any step, the ladder pauses/retreats.
7. **Free-cancellation rate** added alongside a cheaper non-refundable one.
8. **Dynamic pricing** (PriceLabs-class, ~$20/listing/mo). Note: the oft-cited Lago Villas
   "92% occ / +48% RevPAR" is a **vendor case study, unverified** — same caveat we apply to Balinest's
   85%. The verified support is market data: top-quartile listings sustain $313+ in this zone.

**Projection — sensitivity, not a promise** (base ~$122/available-night rests on the operator's
*unverified* 85% claim; conditioned on the Guesty pull):
| True occupancy → | 55% | 70% | 85% |
|---|---|---|---|
| ADR +20% ($173) | $95 | $121 | $147 |
| ADR +40% ($202) | $111 | $141 | $172 |
Floor rule: occupancy below the agreed floor pauses the ladder. If real occupancy is near market
(~50%), the problem is demand, not rate — sequencing shifts to distribution/brand first.

## 10. Review equity — platform-correct (previous wording was dangerous)
- **Never delete or re-create listing IDs.** Airbnb reviews are welded to the listing; Booking reviews
  to the property ID (and expire ~36 months). "Transferring" reviews is mechanically impossible.
- **Rename in place** — safe on both platforms.
- Airbnb: the transferable asset is the **host profile** — run all units under one Superhost account
  so the aggregate badge/rating halos new units; accept new listings start at zero and plan the
  first-10-reviews push (opening rate + review asks).
- Booking: add new units as **room types under the existing scored property page** where physically
  the same complex — the one legitimate consolidation.
- Start the review flow on whichever Google surface §2 verification allows; otherwise OTA reviews.

## 12. Budget, KPIs & the lag warning (was missing — unapprovable without it)
**Budget (test scale):** site build $2–5k one-off · photo/video $1–2k · Google Ads $600–1,700/mo
(brand cap $100–200 + generic) · Meta/TikTok $1,000–2,000/mo · PriceLabs ~$20/listing/mo · content

near-zero cost, payback in weeks.
**KPIs:** direct share % · RevPAR index vs market · ADR by unit · 30-day pickup pace · CPA per booked
night (target ≤ the $30–45 commission displaced) · Booking score trajectory (8.7 → 9.0+) · review
velocity · booking-engine CVR.
**⚠ Results lag, stated plainly:** target guests book **60–180 days out**. Paid spend in month 1
produces stays in months 3–7. Days 0–90 are judged on **leading indicators** (CTR, engine sessions,
WhatsApp starts, forward-dated bookings on the pace calendar); realized ADR/occupancy impact reads at
days 90–270. An owner not told this will rationally kill the program at exactly the wrong moment.
