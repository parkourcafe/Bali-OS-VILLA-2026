§1 · Who comes

Surfers own the identity, couples & luxury own the volume

Surfers — identityCouples / honeymoon — volumeLuxury — volume, growingDigital nomads — growthWellness / yoga — growthParty / families — minor

Origins: Australia dominant (~25% of arrivals + surf heritage), then India · China · South Korea · UK · France · US · Singapore. Nomads skew Brazil/Portugal/Europe. 2024→26 shift: toward Asian markets, a rising Russian cohort, and longer villa stays over hotels Med — favorable to villa inventory.

§2 · The key finding

Two journeys into Uluwatu — opposite reasons

Leisure tourists — Uluwatu is the FINALE High
Canggu/Seminyak (arrival, social) → Ubud (culture) → Uluwatu last as the scenic wind-down before flying out (airport is close). They don't relocate away from crowds — they sequence Uluwatu at the end.
Nomads — RELOCATE from Canggu Med-High
The classic "left Canggu because too crowded" move, mid-trip, for a slower pace. Surfers & retreat guests base in Uluwatu the whole time.

§6 · Complaints & the 2024→26 shift

What frustrates them — and the warning sign

Top complaints (ranked)
  1. Need a scooter — spread out, not walkable Very high
  2. Isolated listings 2–6km from the beach
  3. Construction noise & dust (2024–26 boom)
  4. Traffic, esp. 4–7pm sunset rush
  5. Steep stairs / tide-dependent beach access
  6. "Boujee" / overpriced clubs & villas
  7. Taxi mafia blocking Grab/Gojek
  8. Photos ≠ reality / misleading location
Sentiment shift — confirmed High

A clear, growing narrative: "Uluwatu is becoming the next Canggu" — over-developed, more crowded, losing charm. Flashpoints: Bingin Beach redevelopment (40+ villas demolished), the "Cube Apartment" project, the coastal road cutting the cliffs above the breaks. Uluwatu's #1 differentiator — "quieter than Canggu" — is actively eroding.

§7 · What this means for Anjuna

Turn the demand truth into moves

  • Target Australians + couples/honeymooners + surfers first; growth from nomads/wellness & Asian markets. Sell the view + private pool + privacy — the exact top-3 things travelers love.
  • Be visible 2–6 months out (AU/EU/honeymoon booking windows) — the Inspiration stage where Anjuna currently has no guest-facing brand.
  • Pre-empt the top objections in listing copy: scooter/transport help, exact walk-times to Thomas Beach, honest construction disclosure + mitigation, airport transfer. These are the market's #1–5 complaints — answering them converts.
  • WhatsApp pre-arrival is the expected norm for Uluwatu villas — reinforces the response-system + owner-brand moves.
  • Risk: the "next Canggu" sentiment + Anjuna's own on-site construction is a live reputation threat — manage it in copy now, not after the reviews land.
